# Problem Statement

## AI RAG Assistant for Quest Analytics

### Why Build an AI RAG Assistant:

Imagine you work as a consultant for **Quest Analytics**, a small but fast-growing research organization.

In today's fast-paced research environment, the sheer volume of scientific papers can be overwhelming, making it nearly impossible to stay up-to-date with the latest developments. 

The researchers at Quest Analytics have been struggling to find the time to examine countless documents, let alone extract the most relevant and insightful information. 

You have been hired to build an AI RAG assistant that can read, understand, and summarize vast amounts of data, all in real time. Construct the AI-powered RAG assistant, which will optimize research endeavors at Quest Analytics.

---

### Project Objectives:

1. **Document Processing**: Build a system capable of ingesting and processing vast amounts of scientific literature and research documents
2. **Real-time Understanding**: Implement AI capabilities to read, comprehend, and extract insights from documents in real-time
3. **Intelligent Summarization**: Develop summarization features that highlight the most relevant and insightful information
4. **Research Optimization**: Create tools that streamline and enhance the research workflow at Quest Analytics
5. **Scalability**: Design the system to handle growing volumes of data as the organization expands

### Target Use Cases:

- **Literature Review**: Quickly scan and summarize recent publications in specific research areas
- **Knowledge Discovery**: Identify connections and patterns across multiple documents
- **Research Assistance**: Provide instant answers to research questions using the knowledge base
- **Document Analysis**: Extract key findings, methodologies, and conclusions from scientific papers
- **Trend Identification**: Discover emerging research trends and developments

### Expected Outcomes:

- Significantly reduced time spent on manual document review
- Enhanced ability to stay current with latest research developments
- Improved research quality through comprehensive literature analysis
- Increased productivity and efficiency for Quest Analytics research team
- Competitive advantage through faster access to relevant information
